Preserve ALL original content: every film, artwork, library item, image URL,

## Big Sur Visual Language (Apple, macOS 11)

- **Materials**: Frosted-glass surfaces (backdrop-filter: blur(20-40px) +
  saturate(180%)) for the navbar and any overlay panels. Semi-translucent
  whites/grays over content, not opaque.
- **Depth**: Layered elevation with soft, diffuse shadows
  (0 20px 40px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)). Nothing flat — everything floats.
- **Radii**: Generous, Apple-scale — 16-24px on cards, 12px on buttons,
  28px+ on hero containers (continuous corner curvature feel).
- **Color**: Soft gradient backgrounds inspired by Big Sur wallpapers
  (warm dusk peaches into lavender into deep blue). Avoid pure #fff or #000.
  Use off-whites (#fafafa) and warm blacks (#1d1d1f).
- **Typography**: SF Pro / Inter. Large, confident headings (SF Pro Display
  weight 600-700, -0.02em tracking). Body copy in SF Pro Text. Generous
  line-height (1.5-1.7).
- **Spacing**: Airy. Big breathing room. Apple-product-page generosity —
  120px+ section padding on desktop.

## Motion & Liveness

- **Scroll-driven parallax** on hero images and artwork cards: images
  translateY at 0.3x-0.6x scroll speed.
- **Spring physics** on hover (cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)) for
  cards and buttons — subtle lift (translateY(-4px)) + shadow expansion.
- **Live breathing** on artwork/film poster images: a very slow
  (8-12s infinite) scale(1.0 -> 1.03) ease-in-out to make statics feel alive.
- **Staggered reveals** on scroll — grid items fade+rise into view with
  60-80ms stagger, IntersectionObserver-driven.
- **Smooth route transitions** — crossfade + slight scale when navigating
  between sections. No hard cuts.
- **Lightbox with spring open/close** — scale from thumbnail bounds
  (FLIP-style) with backdrop blur ramp-in.
- **Gradient mesh background** that slowly drifts (CSS @keyframes, 30s+)
  behind the hero, evoking the Big Sur dusk wallpaper.

## Constraints

- Use real image URLs from jamesbuckhouse.com. Never substitute placeholders.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion — disable breathing and parallax when set.
- Must hit 60fps on a 2020 MacBook Air (transform/opacity only, no
  layout-triggering properties in animations).
- Plain HTML/CSS/JS (matches the existing scaffold). No React.
- Dark mode isn't required, but the palette should feel like late-afternoon
  Big Sur light — warm, cinematic.
